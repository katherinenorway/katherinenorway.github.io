Reasons to visit Norway

Norway is a beautiful country located on the Scandinavian peninsula. It is full of rich history and beautiful landscapes, making it irresistable to visit.

Norwegian Fjords are absolutely stunning.

Norway was declared a land of happiness by the World Happiness Index. Research scientists ranked it at the top out of 157 countries due to it many attractions.

Norway overflows with rich culture and history.

The landscapes are beautiful not only for photgraphy but also painting. Visiting the country allows one to experience these beauties firsthand. One can capture the mystical fjords and dazzling hills with oils or watercolors.

Oslo

If the vivid countryside just isn't for you, consider the city of Oslo, filled with many attractions.

  • Norway's National Gallery
  • The Norwegian National Opera and Ballet
  • Vigeland Sculpture Park

Landscape

Visiting Norway is a once in a lifetime experience. It offers so many beutiful sites. Even spending a few days to a week would be worth the travel.

Edvard

Norway was home to not only Edvard Grieg, but also Edvard Munch. It would be an honor to visit their astonishingly beauiful homeland.

With such a biodiverse land, Norway offers a great escape from American polluted cities.

Overall, Norway is the perfect country to visit in the summer. It holds so many opportunities to see cultural art and the raw environment.
